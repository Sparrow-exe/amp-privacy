Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 9, 2025

AMP Diagnostics (“we”, “us”, or “our”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

AMP Diagnostics collects telemetry data about your device and network, including but not limited to:

  • Device model and operating system
  • Network configuration and performance
  • Wi-Fi SSID and signal strength
  • IP address and connection statistics

No personal identifiers such as your name, email, or location are collected unless explicitly provided for support purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data solely to help diagnose connectivity issues, evaluate device performance, and improve the AMP Diagnostics service. Your data may also be used to verify successful telemetry uploads and monitor system health.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties. Data is transmitted securely to our servers and is used only for internal diagnostics and support.

4. Data Storage and Security

All telemetry data is transmitted and stored using standard networking protocols. We take reasonable measures to store data securely and restrict access to authorized systems and personnel only.

5. Your Rights

You may request deletion of any collected data by contacting us at the email address below. However, keep in mind that AMP Diagnostics is primarily used in managed device environments, and data deletion may depend on your organization's policies.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted to this page with an updated effective date.
